Welcome to Nethra

Compassionate Living Designed for Senior Well-Being

At Nethra Senior Care, we believe that aging should be graceful, dignified, and joyful. Our assisted living community blends medical support, emotional well-being, social engagement, and daily comfort into one nurturing experience.

From medication management and nutritious meals to community events and rehabilitation support, we provide everything seniors need to feel safe, valued, and truly at home.
